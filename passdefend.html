Why use PassDefend?

Secure local storage

Credentials are encrypted and stored locally instead of being stored on a companies servers, like most other password managers

Breach checking

PassDefend can securely check passwords against the HaveIBeenPwned database to detect if the password has been included in a data breach

Password generation

Generate extremely secure passwords to your own specifications using our password generation engine, to help protect your accounts from the most common password attacks

Colour schemes

Make it your own with a choice of 4 premade colour schemes to match your preferred style, with each style perfectly complementing your desktop

Modern, easy to use design

PassDefend is designed from the ground up to look modern on your machine and be easy to use; from its sleek rounded look, to its modern flat design and accompanying icon buttons

Easy migration

Import the database from your old password manager directly into PassDefend to get running straight away! (Feature coming soon)
